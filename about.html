About Kasselsvlei Primary

Kasselsvlei Primary is a no-fee school committed to providing quality education to the children of Bellville South. Situated in the heart of the community, we strive to create a safe, supportive, and engaging learning environment where every child can reach their full potential. Our dedicated teachers are passionate about academic excellence, personal growth, and the holistic development of our learners. At Kasselsvlei Primary, we believe that education is the foundation for a brighter future, and we work closely with parents and the community to empower our pupils for success.
